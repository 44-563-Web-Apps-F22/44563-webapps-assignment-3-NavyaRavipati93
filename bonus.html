Red Dragon - Neon fuchsia

  • The Cortland Red Dragons football program is the intercollegiate American football team for the State University of New York at Cortland
  • The team competes in the NCAA Division III and is a member of the Empire 8. The team plays its home games at SUNY Cortland Stadium Complex in Cortland, New York
  • The Red Dragons led by Curt Fitzpatrick, who has served as head coach since 2020.
